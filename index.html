Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JerseyCTF

What is JerseyCTF?

JerseyCTF is a beginner-friendly Capture the Flag competition hosted by NJIT ACM, NICC, and SCI. It offers jeopardy-style challenges in cryptography, forensics, web, and more, plus talks from industry and government experts.

How can I compete?

You can compete individually or as part of a team. Simply register through our website, create an account on our CTF platform. Must be 18+ to attend in-person.

What do I need to compete?

All you need is an internet-connected device to compete and watch talks. Using Linux distros like Kali or Parrot OS is recommended for their built-in tools. They're free and can run on virtualization software.

How big is JerseyCTF?

JerseyCTF had 1,700+ registrations in 2024. We are looking forward to welcoming more registrants and participants for JerseyCTF V in 2025!

Will there be prizes?

Yes! There are 2 leaderboard divisions: Student and Open. Prizes will be awarded in both divisions for top scoring teams and participants residing in the United States will receive a complimentary JerseyCTF t-shirt.
