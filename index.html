Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JerseyCTF

What is JerseyCTF?

JerseyCTF is an annual cybersecurity capture the flag competition where participants solve challenges across various security domains including cryptography, web exploitation, forensics, and more.

How can I compete?

You can compete individually or as part of a team. Simply register through our website, create an account on our CTF platform, and you'll be ready to participate when the competition starts.

What do I need to compete?

A computer with internet access, basic cybersecurity knowledge, and tools like Wireshark, Python, or Kali Linux. Beginners are welcome too - we have challenges for all skill levels.

How big is JerseyCTF?

JerseyCTF has grown to become one of the largest student-run CTF competitions in the United States, with thousands of participants from across the globe joining each year.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! We offer prizes for top performers across different categories and divisions. Prizes include cash rewards, tech gadgets, swag, and opportunities to connect with industry professionals.
